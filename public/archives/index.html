Making a map (or my first blogpost)

 Jan 1, 2018
Getting the data A couple of months ago I discovered a package developed by Public Health England that makes it easier to extract the data behind their [fingertips website] (www.fingertips.phe.gov.uk). I wanted to try it and as I like maps I decided to download some data at CCG level and make an interactive choropleth map using the leaflet package. I use select_indicators to browse the indicators and pick one at random that seems to have CCG level data.
